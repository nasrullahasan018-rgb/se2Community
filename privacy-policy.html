1. Introduction

SE2 Global Commerce Ltd. ("SE2," "we," "us," "our," or "Company") operates the SE2 - Suite Explore 2 e-commerce platform (the "Platform" or "Website"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a transparent understanding of how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information.

This Privacy Policy explains our data handling practices for users ("you," "your," or "user") who visit, browse, register, purchase, or interact with our Platform and services. By accessing, using, or registering on SE2, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by this Privacy Policy.

⚠️ Important: This policy complies with GDPR (EU), CCPA (California), CalOPPA, and international privacy standards. If you do not agree with our practices, please discontinue using our Platform.

2. Information We Collect

We collect various types of information to provide, improve, and personalize our services. This information is collected through multiple channels:

2.1 Personal Information

When you create an account, place an order, or contact us, we collect:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Date of birth (optional)
  • Gender (optional)
  • Profile picture (optional)
  • Username and password

2.2 Account Information

To maintain your account, we collect and store:

  • Account creation date and time
  • Account status (active, suspended, deleted)
  • Account preferences and settings
  • Communication preferences
  • Account authentication methods
  • Two-factor authentication details (if enabled)

2.3 Payment Information

When you make purchases, we collect payment-related data:

  • Credit/Debit card details (processed via secure payment gateways only)
  • Bank account information (for certain payment methods)
  • Digital wallet information
  • Billing address
  • Payment transaction history and status
  • Refund and chargeback information

ℹ️ Note: We do NOT store full credit card numbers. All payment processing is handled by PCI DSS-compliant third-party payment gateways (Stripe, PayPal, 2Checkout, etc.).

2.4 Order Information

When you place orders, we collect:

  • Product names, descriptions, and quantities
  • Order date and time
  • Order value and pricing details
  • Shipping address
  • Delivery tracking information
  • Order status and history
  • Return and refund requests
  • Product reviews and ratings
  • Wishlist and saved items

2.5 Device & Browser Information

We automatically collect technical information about your device and browsing:

  • IP address
  • Device type (smartphone, tablet, desktop)
  • Operating system and version
  • Browser type and version
  • Device identifiers and UDID
  • Mobile carrier information
  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Referring website URL
  • Search queries
  • Click stream data

2.6 Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use the following tracking technologies:

Cookies

Session cookies (temporary) and persistent cookies (long-term) for authentication, preferences, and analytics.

Web Beacons

1x1 pixel images to track email opens and engagement.

Pixels & Tags

Marketing and conversion tracking pixels from Google, Facebook, and other platforms.

Local Storage

Browser local storage and session storage for user preferences and data.

2.7 Communication Data

When you contact us, we collect:

  • Email content and attachments
  • Chat transcripts
  • Phone call recordings (with your consent)
  • Support ticket information
  • Customer feedback and survey responses
  • Social media messages and comments

2.8 Location Data

We may collect your location through:

  • GPS coordinates (if you enable location services)
  • IP-based location approximation
  • Shipping address information
  • Mobile app location permissions

4. Legal Basis for Processing Data

Under GDPR and similar regulations, we process your data based on the following legal grounds:

Consent - Processing based on your explicit permission +

We process data with your explicit consent for:

  • Marketing emails and promotional communications
  • Analytics and tracking (via cookies)
  • Location-based services
  • Personalized recommendations

Your Right: You can withdraw consent at any time by updating your preferences or contacting us.

Contract Performance - Processing necessary to fulfill your order +

We process data as necessary to:

  • Create and maintain your account
  • Process your orders and payments
  • Deliver products and services
  • Handle refunds and returns
  • Provide customer support

Note: Without this processing, we cannot provide our services to you.

Legitimate Interest - Processing for our business needs +

We process data for legitimate business interests including:

  • Platform improvement and optimization
  • Fraud detection and prevention
  • Personalization and analytics
  • Direct marketing (subject to your preferences)
  • Business intelligence and reporting

Balancing Test: We balance our interests against your privacy rights and provide opt-out options.

Legal Obligation - Processing required by law +

We may process data when required by:

  • Tax and accounting regulations
  • Anti-fraud and anti-money laundering (AML) laws
  • KYC (Know Your Customer) requirements
  • Court orders or government requests
  • Data protection and consumer protection laws

8. Data Security Measures

We implement industry-leading security measures to protect your information:

8.1 Encryption & Transmission Security

  • HTTPS/TLS: All data transmissions encrypted with SSL/TLS 1.2 or higher
  • End-to-End Encryption: Sensitive data encrypted at rest and in transit
  • Database Encryption: AES-256 encryption for stored data
  • Password Encryption: bcrypt or Argon2 hashing (never plain text)

8.2 Access Controls & Authentication

  • Role-based access control (RBAC)
  • Two-factor authentication (2FA) available
  • Multi-factor authentication (MFA) for admin accounts
  • IP whitelisting and VPN requirements
  • Session timeout and automatic logout

8.3 Network & Infrastructure Security

  • Firewalls and intrusion detection systems (IDS)
  • DDoS protection and mitigation
  • Web application firewalls (WAF)
  • Regular security patches and updates
  • Penetration testing and vulnerability assessments

8.4 Employee & Vendor Security

  • Background checks for all employees
  • Data protection training mandatory
  • Non-disclosure agreements (NDAs)
  • Vendor security assessments
  • Limited access to personal data

8.5 Incident Response & Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach or security incident, we will:

  • Notify affected users within 72 hours (GDPR requirement)
  • Provide details of the breach and remediation steps
  • Offer free credit monitoring or identity protection services if applicable
  • Cooperate with law enforcement and regulators
  • Document incidents for audit purposes

Note: While we employ robust security measures, no system is 100% secure. We encourage you to use strong passwords and enable 2FA on your account.

9. User Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

9.1 Right to Access Data

You have the right to request a copy of all personal data we hold about you. To exercise this right:

  • Log in to your account and download your data from the Account Settings
  • Email us at se2.support.community@gmail.com with "Data Access Request" in the subject
  • We will provide your data in a portable, machine-readable format (JSON/CSV) within 30 days

9.2 Right to Correct/Update Data

You can update your information directly in your account settings anytime. If you cannot access certain data, contact us for assistance.

9.3 Right to Delete Data (Right to be Forgotten)

You can request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal and contractual obligations:

  • Log in and delete your account from Account Settings
  • Your data will be soft-deleted (30-day recovery period)
  • After 30 days, your data is permanently deleted from active systems
  • Exception: Tax and legal records retained as required by law

9.4 Right to Restrict Processing

You can request that we limit how we use your data while a dispute is resolved or a deletion request is pending.

9.5 Right to Data Portability

You can request your data in a structured, commonly-used, machine-readable format (such as JSON or CSV) to transfer to another service.

9.6 Right to Withdraw Consent

If we're processing your data based on consent, you can withdraw it anytime:

  • Update email preferences to unsubscribe from marketing
  • Disable cookies and tracking from browser settings
  • Contact us to opt-out of specific processing activities

9.7 Right to Objection

You can object to processing of your data for direct marketing, profiling, or automated decision-making.

9.8 Right to Lodge a Complaint

If you believe we're mishandling your data, you can:

  • Contact our Data Protection Officer (DPO) at dpo@se2.com
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority (DPA)

We monitor for fraudulent activity using:

  • Machine learning algorithms for anomaly detection

  • Device fingerprinting and behavioral analysis
  • Third-party fraud detection services (Sift, Kount, etc.)

16.2 Data Used for Fraud Prevention

  • Payment and billing information
  • Device and IP information

16.3 Actions Taken for Suspected Fraud
